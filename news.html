The 27th Annual CFA Allbreed Cat Show

Mesa, AZ December 14 - 15, 2019

Phoenix Feline Fanciers annually hosts the largest CFA allbreed cat show in Arizona in December. The show is extremely popular with professional exhibitors from all over the world, vendors and cat-loving spectators in the greater Phoenix area and beyond Arizonaâ€™s borders.

Next year's show, one of the 10 largest CFA shows worldwide, will take place on December 14-15, 2019 at the Mesa Arizona Convention Center, 263 N. Center Street, Mesa, AZ 85201. The show will feature not only 350 pedigreed cats and kittens and their owners, but vendors with feline related merchandise for sale. Household Pets will be featured. Visitors will have lots of opportunities to watch the judges evaluate the cats, plus meet the breeders and exhibitors. Veteran cats, 7 years and older, will have their own judging. Rescue cats from the All About Animals Rescue organization will also be available for adoption.

For the 2019 show, exhibitors can exercise their creative talents with a cat costume contest and a cage decorating contest.

All entries in the show will be evaluated by judges licensed by The Cat Fanciers' Association, and there will be 10 separate judging rings. In addition, special classes will be held for Household Pet cats and Veterans. Special trophies are awarded to the highest scoring cats in Championship and Premiership. Rosettes will be presented to the highest scoring Kitten, Veteran and Household Pet. Be sure to check out our photo gallery from previous shows!

Join us, and see what it is all about!
